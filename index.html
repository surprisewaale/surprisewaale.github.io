Karishma Malhotra

Founder

An avid marketer with a beaming ray of self-confidence is what defines this young and bright entrepreneur. Having a very innovative bent of mind from the very beginning Karishma was always attracted to the industries that involved creativity, this in turn led her to start a career in advertising and PR.

After attaining a master's degree in Marketing from Kings College London she moved back to India and got linked to all the leading media across the country. She also got associated with brands such as Sunburn, Lakme Fashion Week, Lakme Salon, Redbull, Dove hair etc. that gave her the industry expertise and she found her passion in creative writing and advertising. To pursue this passion further Karishma decided to start with an agency called Urban Branding that employs some of the most creative minds and simply believes in the mantra of “untamed branding”.

“When it comes to creativity, there shouldn't be any restrictions to the thought process and we maintain a work environment where in the flow of ideas and thoughts remains pure, unaltered and original. The industry is dynamic and following old methods of advertising can prove to be a curse for the clients, we at Urban Branding use the most updated methods to create a brand or sustain a brand image” says Karishma Malhotra.
